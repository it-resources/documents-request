Document Requirements
1. Upload the required documents.
  • Copy of the student/requestor's government-issued ID, preferably passport with three (3) signatures beside it.
  • Photo/Selfie of the requestor holding the ID.
  • Special Power of Attorney (SPA) for those sending their representatives to process the request and receive the printed school credential(s)/record(s).
  • Copy of valid government-issued ID of the authorized representative or attorney in-fact signed with three (3) signatures beside it.
  • For those requesting for the 2nd copy of their Diploma, a duly notarized affidavit of loss of the original document. The original hard copy of the notarized affidavit of loss should be submitted to Registrar's office personally or through courier before release of the requested diploma.
  • Kindly upload PNG, JPG or JPEG files only.
